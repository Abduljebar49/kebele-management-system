Our Vision
The situation related to auditing; Calculating annual and monthly budget is time consuming, and boring. Beside this it is almost inaccurate. Next, it is difficult to determine the income and expense of the kebele at instant of time. Most of time this leads to corruption, which in turn cause extravagancy of the budget and abuse of some workers in budgeting system
